    Smooth Mollifier¶

    ipctk.f0_SF(x: float, epsv_times_h: float) → float¶

    Smooth friction mollifier function.

    \[f_0(y)= \begin{cases} -\frac{x^3}{3\epsilon_v^2 h^2} + \frac{x^2}{\epsilon_vh} + \frac{\epsilon_v h}{3}, & |x| \in\left[0, \epsilon_v h\right) \newline x, & |x| \geq \epsilon_v h \end{cases}\]
    Parameters:¶
    x: float¶

    The tangential relative speed.

    epsv_times_h: float¶

    Mollifier parameter \(\epsilon_v h\).

    Returns:¶

    The value of the mollifier function at x.

    ipctk.f1_SF_over_x(x: float, epsv_times_h: float) → float¶

    Compute the derivative of f0_SF divided by x (\(\frac{f_0'(x)}{x}\)).

    \[f_1(x) = f_0'(x) = \begin{cases} -\frac{x^2}{\epsilon_v^2 h^2}+\frac{2 x}{\epsilon_v h}, & |x| \in\left[0, \epsilon_v h \right) \newline 1, & |x| \geq h \epsilon_v \end{cases}\]
    \[\frac{f_1(x)}{x} = \begin{cases} -\frac{x}{\epsilon_v^2 h^2}+\frac{2}{\epsilon_v h}, & |x| \in\left[0, \epsilon_v h \right) \newline \frac{1}{x}, & |x| \geq h \epsilon_v \end{cases}\]
    Parameters:¶
    x: float¶

    The tangential relative speed.

    epsv_times_h: float¶

    Mollifier parameter \(\epsilon_v h\).

    Returns:¶

    The value of the derivative of f0_SF divided by x.

    ipctk.df1_x_minus_f1_over_x3(x: float, epsv_times_h: float) → float¶

    The derivative of f1 times x minus f1 all divided by x cubed.

    \[\frac{f_1'(x) x - f_1(x)}{x^3} = \begin{cases} -\frac{1}{x \epsilon_v^2 h^2}, & |x| \in\left[0, \epsilon_v h \right) \newline -\frac{1}{x^3}, & |x| \geq h \epsilon_v \end{cases}\]
    Parameters:¶
    x: float¶

    The tangential relative speed.

    epsv_times_h: float¶

    Mollifier parameter \(\epsilon_v h\).

    Returns:¶

    The derivative of f1 times x minus f1 all divided by x cubed.

    ipctk.point_triangle_tangent_basis(p: numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 1]], t0: numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 1]], t1: numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 1]], t2: numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 1]]) → numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 2]]¶

    Compute a basis for the space tangent to the point-triangle pair.

    \[\begin{bmatrix} \frac{t_1 - t_0}{\|t_1 - t_0\|} & \frac{((t_1 - t_0)\times(t_2 - t_0)) \times(t_1 - t_0)}{\|((t_1 - t_0)\times(t_2 - t_0))\times(t_1 - t_0)\|} \end{bmatrix}\]
    Parameters:¶
    p: numpy.ndarray[numpy.float64[3, 1]]¶

    Point
